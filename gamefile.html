üéØ Objective

You're an Israelite journeying to Jerusalem to make your annual sacrifice. After disaster strikes your prepared offerings, you must earn new sacrificial offerings along the way. First player to reach 40 Sacrifice Points wins!

üé≤ Basic Gameplay

  • Players start at "Your Village" with 0 Sacrifice Points (SP)
  • Roll the die to move around the board clockwise
  • Answer trivia questions at biblical locations to earn SP
  • Redeem SP for livestock and coins as offerings
  • Pass "Your Village" to gain 5 SP

üìö Trivia Questions

  • Correct Answer: 3 SP
  • With Helper Hint: 2 SP (if you have that character as a helper)
  • Don't Know: 1 SP
  • Each question can only be answered once per game
  • Answered questions become greyed out

üë• Helpers

  • Answer 3 questions from the same biblical character to recruit them as a Helper
  • Helpers provide hints for questions at their locations
  • Only one player can have each Helper - first to recruit them claims them permanently
  • Other players can still answer that character's questions for SP, but won't earn Helper points
